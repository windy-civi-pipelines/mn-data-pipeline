No. 27, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 7, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2honoring Steven Strom on receiving the 2025 Star of Life Award from the 1.3Minnesota Ambulance Association. 1.4WHEREAS, the Minnesota Ambulance Association was established in 1960 to promote 1.5excellence and quality in the ambulance industry in Minnesota; and 1.6WHEREAS, the organization created the Star of Life Award, the state's highest recognition 1.7for emergency medical services (EMS) professionals, to honor individuals who go above and beyond 1.8in delivering life-saving care, mentoring the next generation, and strengthening the EMS system; 1.9and 1.10WHEREAS, Steven Strom of Albert Lea has worked in emergency medical services for 1.11nearly two years and in that short time has fully embraced the attitude of putting patients' needs 1.12first as an emergency medical technician with Mayo Clinic Ambulance Service; and 1.13WHEREAS, an example of that commitment was when Steven reunited a patient requiring 1.14medical care with their spouse. When the patient expressed a desire to be with their loved one 1.15during their final hours, Steven worked to bring the couple back together once the patient had 1.16received necessary treatment; and 1.17WHEREAS, Steven exemplifies what it means to be an excellent all-around emergency 1.18medical technician. He is a team member his peers know they can always count on, and Steven's 1.19empathy, dedication to putting patients' needs first, and teamwork make him a shining example of 1.20a complete EMS clinician; and 1.21WHEREAS, Steven's family has a strong tradition of public service. His father works in law 1.22enforcement and his sister works as a 911 dispatcher; and 1.23WHEREAS, outside of work, Steven enjoys the outdoors, movies, traveling, and video 1.24games; and 1.25NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.26congratulates Steven Strom on receiving the Star of Life Award from the Minnesota Ambulance 1.27Association and commends him for his exemplary service as an emergency medical technician. 1.28BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.29enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.30Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Steven Strom. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration Carla J.
