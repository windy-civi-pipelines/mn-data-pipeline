No. 53, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on May 19, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2wishing longtime mental health practitioner Ted Matthews well in retirement. 1.3WHEREAS, Ted Matthews served the people of Minnesota as a mental health practitioner 1.4for decades; and 1.5WHEREAS, working as a rural mental health counselor, Ted directly improved the lives of 1.6countless farmers by providing tireless assistance to those in need across our state; and 1.7WHEREAS, in addition to directly providing mental health care, Ted shared his experience 1.8to educate the public about mental health, and in particular the challenges facing farmers and rural 1.9communities; and 1.10WHEREAS, Ted made an enduring, positive impact on our state; and 1.11WHEREAS, Ted has decided to enjoy a well-earned retirement; and 1.12NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.13thanks Ted Matthews for his decades of service to the people of Minnesota, honors his lasting 1.14contribution to our state, and congratulates him on his retirement. 1.15BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.16enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.17Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Ted Matthews.
