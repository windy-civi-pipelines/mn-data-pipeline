No. 19, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on March 17, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2honoring the Dodge County Wildcats girls hockey team on its state 1.3championship. 1.4WHEREAS, the Dodge County Wildcats girls hockey team triumphed in the Minnesota 1.5State High School League Class A girls hockey championship, securing a hard-fought victory in 1.6an overtime game; and 1.7WHEREAS, the Wildcats' passion for the game and determination were evident throughout 1.8their remarkable playoff run; and 1.9WHEREAS, the Wildcats showcased resilience and perseverance, responding swiftly to 1.10challenges and maintaining offensive pressure throughout the game, ultimately securing the 1.11championship; and 1.12WHEREAS, the Wildcats' goaltender played a pivotal role in the victory, making key saves 1.13early in the game to keep the team in contention; and 1.14WHEREAS, despite the challenges faced throughout the game, the Wildcats' teamwork and 1.15resolve led to their triumph, sealing the championship with a decisive goal in overtime; and 1.16WHEREAS, the leadership demonstrated by the Wildcats throughout the season and in this 1.17championship game exemplifies the values of hard work, perseverance, and sportsmanship; and 1.18NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.19congratulates the Wildcats on their state championship. 1.20BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.21enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.22Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to the Dodge County 1.23Wildcats girls hockey team. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration Carla J.
