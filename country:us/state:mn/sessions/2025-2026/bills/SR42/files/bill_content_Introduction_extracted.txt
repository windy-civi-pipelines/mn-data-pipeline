No. 42, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on May 8, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2recognizing Dave Renner for his accomplishments over a decades-long career 1.3in advocacy, education, and service to the physicians and patients of 1.4Minnesota. 1.5WHEREAS, Dave Renner began his career as a staffer at the Minnesota Senate in the 1983 1.6legislative session; and 1.7WHEREAS, Dave first joined the Minnesota Medical Association (MMA) in 1988, on whose 1.8behalf he has lobbied for over 36 years; and 1.9WHEREAS, he has advocated to advance the MMA's mission to make Minnesota the 1.10healthiest state in the nation and the best place to practice medicine; and 1.11WHEREAS, he has demonstrated the best characteristics of a successful advocate, carrying 1.12himself with integrity, expansive subject matter knowledge, and a deep commitment to the public 1.13good; and 1.14WHEREAS, Dave will retire on July 1, 2025, leaving behind a legacy of accomplishments 1.15that have helped to improve health and health care in Minnesota; and 1.16NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.17commends Dave Renner for his dedication and congratulations, appreciation, and best wishes are 1.18extended to him from those who have had the pleasure of working with him. 1.19BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.20enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.21Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Dave Renner.
