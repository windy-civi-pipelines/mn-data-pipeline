No. 23, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 3, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2congratulating Benjamin Nordmann for earning the rank of Eagle Scout. 1.3WHEREAS, the Eagle Scout rank is the highest rank earned in the Boy Scouts of America; 1.4and 1.5WHEREAS, attaining the rank of Eagle Scout in Scouts BSA is an important achievement 1.6that is worthy of recognition; and 1.7WHEREAS, Benjamin Nordmann is a member of the Boy Scouts of America and has 1.8displayed the attributes of a responsible and serving member; and 1.9WHEREAS, Benjamin has demonstrated mastery of all Scouting skills and has completed 1.10a community service project to fulfill all requirements necessary to attain the rank of Eagle Scout. 1.11The project, which involved raising money to buy supplies for the Tri-County Humane Society by 1.12hosting a large garage sale, raised $1,410. The funds were used to purchase necessary supplies, and 1.13Benjamin also promoted animal adoption by distributing information at local public events; and 1.14WHEREAS, an Eagle Court of Honor will be held; and 1.15NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.16congratulates Benjamin Nordmann for attaining the rank of Eagle Scout. 1.17BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare a 1.18copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the Chair of the 1.19Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Benjamin Nordmann. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration Jeff R.
