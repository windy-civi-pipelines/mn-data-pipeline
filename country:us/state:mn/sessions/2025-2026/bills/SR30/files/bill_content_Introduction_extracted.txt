No. 30, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 9, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2congratulating the Albany High School boys basketball team on winning the 1.32025 State High School Class AA boys basketball championship. 1.4WHEREAS, competitive sports in our high schools are helpful in teaching the principles of 1.5sportsmanship and fair play, thereby contributing to better citizenship; and 1.6WHEREAS, the Albany basketball team participated in the 2025 State High School Class 1.7AA boys basketball tournament on March 18-22 at Williams Arena in Minneapolis; and 1.8WHEREAS, the Albany Huskies defeated the Waseca Blue Jays 70-63 in the final game of 1.9the tournament to win the state championship; and 1.10WHEREAS, Albany finished the season with an unblemished 32-0 record; and 1.11NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that 1.12congratulations are extended to the members of the Albany High School boys basketball team for 1.13their sportsmanship and skill. 1.14BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.15enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.16Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to the principal of Albany 1.17High School.
