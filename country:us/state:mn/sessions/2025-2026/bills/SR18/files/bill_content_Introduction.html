S.R. No. 18, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on March 13, 2025




1.1A Senate resolution

1.2congratulating  the Owatonna High School girls gymnastics  team on winning 

1.3the 2025   State High School Class AA girls  gymnastics championship.

1.4WHEREAS, competitive sports in our high schools are helpful in teaching the principles of
         

1.5sportsmanship and fair play, thereby contributing to better citizenship; and

 1.6WHEREAS, the Owatonna gymnastics team, with a deep roster of 13 members, participated  

1.7in the 2025 State High School Class AA girls   gymnastics meet at Roy Wilkins Auditorium  on 

1.8February 21-22, 2025; and

 1.9WHEREAS, Owatonna had been competitive in recent years, finishing in third place in 

1.102019, 2021, and 2023 in the team competition; and

1.11WHEREAS, this year, led by first-place finishes in the uneven bars and vault, the Huskies,
         

1.12with an overall score of 146.825, narrowly defeated second-place New Prague (146.450) and 

1.13third-place Cretin-Derham Hall (146.425) to win their first state championship; and

 1.14NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that 

1.15congratulations are extended to the members of the Owatonna  High School girls gymnastics   team 

1.16for their sportsmanship and skill.

 1.17BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 

1.18enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature
         and that of the 

1.19Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to the principal
         of Owatonna 

1.20High School.

 
         

            
            
            
            

               
               Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate
               Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration
            

         

         

            
            
            
            

               
               
               John R. JasinskiState Senator, District 19
