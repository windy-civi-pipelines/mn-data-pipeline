No. 41, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on May 7, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2recognizing May 9, 2025, as Family Child Care Provider Appreciation Day 1.3and May as the month of the Family Child Care Provider. 1.4WHEREAS, Minnesota has the second highest rate of working mothers in the nation requiring 1.5quality child care that is both accessible and affordable; and 1.6WHEREAS, children need safe and loving environments to learn and grow while their 1.7parents work or attend school; and 1.8WHEREAS, approximately 10,600 licensed family child care providers open their hearts 1.9and homes to Minnesota's children each day, offering an environment that meets the social, 1.10emotional, and cultural needs of the children and families they serve; and 1.11WHEREAS, Minnesota's licensed family child care providers are dedicated professionals 1.12who operate small businesses that provide an invaluable service which is integral to the economic 1.13growth and prosperity of our state and communities; and 1.14WHEREAS, licensed family child care providers make many personal sacrifices to provide 1.15the absolute best care to Minnesota's children, offering an exceptional early education that prepares 1.16them for school and life; and 1.17WHEREAS, Kids-R-It Child Care Center and Preschool is quality early childhood education 1.18and a valuable investment in our children's future; and 1.19WHEREAS, Kids-R-It program directors Pam Duffy and Darcy Spies and teachers Nohemy 1.20Aguirre Alvardo, Miah Bootsma, Lizbeth Comparan, Melissa Contreras, Jasmine Dahlsten, 1.21Boonprapa Ektanitphong, Alida Elliot, Sarh Jauregui, Eh Lar, Samber Lin, Kalyn Mick, Maggie 1.22Nystrom, Maria Perez, Lolita Ramirez Velasquez, Emily Soto Gonzalez, Nevaeh Soto, Halie 1.23Spessard, Kristy Stanley, and Christina VanHorsen are dedicated professionals operating a four-star 1.24Parent Aware Rated program. Kids-R-It recognizes its important role in cultivating, supporting, 1.25and educating young children in a warm, loving, and culturally competent environment; and 1.26NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that 1.27May 9, 2025, be recognized as Family Child Care Provider Appreciation Day, and the month of 1.28May be recognized as the Month of the Family Child Care Provider. We call on Minnesotans to 1.29honor family child care providers and thank them for the contributions they make to our children 1.30and the state. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P.
