No. 24, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 7, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2honoring Hillary Grodevant on receiving the 2025 Star of Life Award from the 1.3Minnesota Ambulance Association. 1.4WHEREAS, the Minnesota Ambulance Association was established in 1960 to promote excellence 1.5and quality in the ambulance industry in Minnesota; and 1.6WHEREAS, the organization created the Star of Life Award, the state's highest recognition for 1.7emergency medical services (EMS) professionals, to honor individuals who go above and beyond in delivering 1.8life-saving care, mentoring the next generation, and strengthening the EMS system; and 1.9WHEREAS, Hillary Grodevant has dedicated her professional career to emergency medical services, 1.10currently serving as an assistant supervisor and paramedic with Mayo Clinic Ambulance Service. Known 1.11for her exceptional skills and unwavering commitment, Hillary has become a beacon of excellence within 1.12her community; and 1.13WHEREAS, Hillary states, "I take pride in the work that I do and am honored to care for our patients, 1.14and enjoy supporting our team"; and 1.15WHEREAS, Hillary grew up in a rural farming community in southeastern Minnesota, where she 1.16began her EMS career 26 years ago as an emergency medical technician. Just three years later, she became 1.17a paramedic and has been with Mayo Clinic Ambulance Service for 23 years. In addition to being a paramedic, 1.18she has also served as a field training officer and an assistant supervisor; and 1.19WHEREAS, in the words of a colleague, "Hillary exemplifies the balance of strong leadership, 1.20fairness, and dedication that makes her an invaluable asset to our team. She objectively approaches every 1.21decision, ensuring patient care and crew utilization are prioritized in the best way possible. Hillary's 1.22professionalism, integrity, and ability to maintain high standards without bias set her apart as a leader. 1.23Whether managing resources, advocating for her team, or stepping in to provide exceptional patient care, 1.24she does so with a quiet diligence that often goes unnoticed. Her work behind the scenes ensuring crews 1.25have the supplies, support, and structure they need makes an immeasurable impact on our operations."; and 1.26WHEREAS, outside of work, Hillary enjoys spending time with her husband and their three cats. 1.27She also enjoys nature, stargazing, old-school video games, riding motorcycles and ATVs, and downhill 1.28skiing; and 1.29NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.30congratulates Hillary Grodevant on receiving the Star of Life Award from the Minnesota Ambulance 1.31Association and commends her for her exemplary service. 1.32BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an enrolled 1.33copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the Chair of the Senate 1.34Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Hillary Grodevant. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration Carla J.
