No. 4, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on March 20, 2025 1.1A Senate concurrent resolution 1.2adopting deadlines for the 2025 regular session. 1.3BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota, the House of Representatives 1.4concurring: 1.5(a) The deadlines in this resolution apply to the 2025 regular session. 1.6(b) The deadlines in this paragraph do not apply to House committees on Capital Investment, 1.7Ways and Means, Taxes, or Rules and Legislative Administration, nor to the Senate committees 1.8on Capital Investment, Finance, Taxes, or Rules and Administration. 1.9(1) The first and second deadlines, Friday, April 4, 2025, at 5:00 p.m., are for committees to 1.10act favorably on all bills other than major appropriation and finance bills. 1.11(2) The third deadline, Friday, April 11, 2025, at 12:00 p.m., is for finance committees to act 1.12favorably on all major appropriation and finance bills. 1.13(c) Major appropriation and finance bills are those bills as defined under Senate Rule 7.3 and 1.14House Rule 4.03(e), and Item 5.2 of the Agreement for House Organization dated February 5, 2025. 1.15(d) When a Senate or House committee acts favorably on a bill after a deadline established 1.16in this resolution, the bill must be referred in the Senate to the Committee on Rules and 1.17Administration or in the House of Representatives to the Committee on Rules and Legislative 1.18Administration for disposition. 1.19(e) The Senate Committee on Rules and Administration or the House Committee on Rules 1.20and Legislative Administration, when reporting a bill referred to the committee in accordance with 1.21this resolution, may waive the application of this resolution to subsequent actions on that bill by 1.22other committees.
