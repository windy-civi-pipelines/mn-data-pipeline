No. 5, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on January 27, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2recognizing Minnesota Senate employees for their years of dedicated service. 1.3WHEREAS, these dedicated employees of the Minnesota Senate have served the State of 1.4Minnesota through a career of professional commitment to state government and are deserving of 1.5special legislative recognition; and 1.6WHEREAS, the employees being recognized for their years of service with the Minnesota 1.7Senate include: 1.8 5 YEARS 1.9 Bean, Fabian Pedersen, Amanda 1.10 Bogan, Shemeka Standish, Jack T. 1.11 Iverson, Nicole Vue, Maikao 1.12 McDonald, Angelique Yeganeh, Jess 1.13 Olmscheid, AJ 1.14 10 YEARS 1.15 Burke, Anna Larson, Andrew 1.16 Clinton, Jacquelyn Muhm, Casey 1.17 Dwyer Bagley, Wendy Vilt, Skyler 1.18 15 YEARS 1.19 Kaplan, Nicole Radunzel, Scott 1.20 Majerus, Ryan Watson, Maureen 1.21 20 YEARS 1.22 Cook, Angela Vesel, Dennis 1.23 Mapes, Melissa 1.24 25 YEARS 1.25 Tupper, Jessica 1.26 30 YEARS 1.27 Lewis, Ann Marie 1.28WHEREAS, the members of the Minnesota Senate value and appreciate the depth of 1.29experience and institutional knowledge these individuals have developed and displayed throughout 1.30their careers; and 2.1WHEREAS, a ceremony recognizing these employees for their years of service will be held 2.2in the Senate Chamber on January 27, 2025; and 2.3NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 2.4commends these employees of the Minnesota Senate for their years of dedication and outstanding 2.5service to the State of Minnesota. 2.6BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 2.7enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 2.8Co-Chairs of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to the Office of the 2.9Secretary of the Senate for public display.
