No. 25, as introduced: 94th Legislative Session (2025-2026) Posted on April 7, 2025 1.1A Senate resolution 1.2honoring Tricia Riggott for receiving the 2025 Star of Life Award from the 1.3Minnesota Ambulance Association. 1.4WHEREAS, the Minnesota Ambulance Association was established in 1960 to promote 1.5excellence and quality in the ambulance industry in Minnesota; and 1.6WHEREAS, the organization created the Star of Life Award, the state's highest recognition 1.7for emergency medical services (EMS) professionals, to honor individuals who go above and beyond 1.8in delivering life-saving care, mentoring the next generation, and strengthening the EMS system; 1.9and 1.10WHEREAS, Tricia Riggott was inspired to begin her career in emergency medical services 1.11by her father, who served as a firefighter and first responder in their hometown of Plymouth, Iowa. 1.12She started working with Mayo Clinic Ambulance Service in 2004 and is currently a critical care 1.13paramedic; and 1.14WHEREAS, she describes her work as profoundly rewarding, particularly her time as a 1.15flight paramedic. In the words of a colleague, "Tricia embodies the very best of our team: dedication, 1.16perseverance, and an unwavering commitment to excellence. She consistently steps up in ways that 1.17go beyond her role. Even in the face of challenges, Tricia remains dedicated, determined, and 1.18focused on what truly matters: delivering the best possible outcomes for our team and the patients 1.19we serve"; and 1.20WHEREAS, Tricia finds her greatest joy and accomplishments as a wife, mother, and 1.21grandmother. She and her husband, Travis, live in small-town southern Minnesota and enjoy boating 1.22on the Mississippi River with friends and family; and 1.23WHEREAS, based on her outstanding qualifications, Tricia was one of 28 professionals to 1.24receive the 2025 Star of Life Award at a ceremony in St. Paul on March 21, 2025; and 1.25NOW, THEREFORE, BE IT RESOLVED by the Senate of the State of Minnesota that it 1.26congratulates Tricia Riggott on receiving the 2025 Star of Life Award from the Minnesota 1.27Ambulance Association and commends her for her exemplary service. 1.28BE IT FURTHER RESOLVED that the Secretary of the Senate is directed to prepare an 1.29enrolled copy of this resolution, to be authenticated by the Secretary's signature and that of the 1.30Chair of the Senate Rules and Administration Committee, and transmit it to Tricia Riggott. Tom BotternSecretary of the Senate Erin P. MurphyChair, Senate Committee onRules and Administration Carla J.
